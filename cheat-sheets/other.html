Writing in RAM memory:

    sudo mount -t tmpfs -o size=2g tmpfs /meteo_storage/untar_tmpfs

Clear cache memory:

    sync && echo 3 > /proc/sys/vm/drop_caches
    or
    /sbin/sysctl -w vm.drop_caches=3

Release memory used by the Linux kernel on caches (From http://www.commandlinefu.com/commands/view/1026/empty-the-linux-buffer-cache):

  • =1 to free pagecache
  • =2 to free dentries and inodes
  • =3 to free pagecache, dentries and inodes

Vim:
